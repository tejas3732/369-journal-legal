369 Journal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

369 Journal is designed as a local-first private journaling app for the 369 manifestation method.

Data Collection

The current version of 369 Journal does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Your active desire, ritual entries, evidence notes, reminder times, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Local Notifications

The app can schedule local reminders for your morning, afternoon, and night writing rituals. Notification permission is optional and can be changed in the app or in iOS Settings. Notification text does not include your private desire.

Export and Delete

You can export your local data from the app settings. You can also delete all local app data from settings, which removes your desire, ritual history, evidence log, and scheduled reminders from this device.

Third Parties

The current version does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, third-party accounts, cloud sync, or server-side storage.

Future Changes

If future versions add cloud sync, accounts, analytics, purchases, AI services, or third-party integrations, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before release.
